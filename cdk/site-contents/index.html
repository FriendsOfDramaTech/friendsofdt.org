Friends of DramaTech

About FoDT

The Friends of DramaTech is the alumni association for DramaTech Theater. Our goal is to support the student members financially, professionally, and socially so that they can further the theater's mission.

Join!

Membership is open to anyone who has ever been a part of DramaTech or who supports the mission of DramaTech.

Click here to join.

For any questions, ideas, or other correspondence e-mail the board.

Contribute

DramaTech's scholarship accounts are administered by the Georgia Tech Foundation. The Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation, and contributions are tax-deductible.

The FoDT board is currently asking that donations be made to the Dull scholarship.

How to Donate

Donations can be made on-line via the Georgia Tech Office of Development's website

  1. Click on the Give Now link at the top.
  2. Enter your gift amount of your gift in the "Donation amount" box.
  3. Enter the amount again in the "Other designation" box.
  4. In the "If other please specify" box, enter "Gay Dull DramaTech Scholarship 997-00-0150."
  5. Once you receive a confirmation email from Georgia Tech, please complete this form so that we can keep track of our progress.

If you run into any problems or have any questions about the process, please reach out to the treasurer.
